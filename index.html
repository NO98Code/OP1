Sheperd's Pie

A shepherd's pie is a savory pie made with minced meat and topped with a layer of mashed potatoes. The filling for the dish is typically cooked in a gravy with onions, peas, celery, and/or carrots. Basically, shepherd's pie is the comfort food to end all comfort foods.

Ingredients

  • 3 large potatoes pealed
  • 1 1/2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 medium chopped onion
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 8 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/2 cup of beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. 1. Boil the potatoes, then mash them
  2. 2. Sautee the onions and add in the vegetables
  3. 3. Add the ground beef, Worcestershire sauce and beef broth
  4. 4. Layer the meat vegetable mixture in a casarole dish
  5. 5. Cover layer with mashed potatoes
  6. 6. Bake on 400 degrees for 30 mins or until golden brown
